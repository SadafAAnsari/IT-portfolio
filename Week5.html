Beware of Password Hackers and Crackers

The world is full of hackers. With the increase in internet technology, the number of hackers has also increased.

A hacker is a person who uses computers to gain unauthorized access to data. Here are some techniques hackers use to crack your passwords:

  • Brute Force
  • Dictionary Attack
  • Social Engineering
  • Phishing
BE AWARE OF HACKERS!!!

Types of Hackers

  • Black Hat Hackers
  • White Hat Hackers
  • Grey Hat Hackers

Hacking Techniques

  1. Social Engineering
  2. Dictionary Attack
  3. Phishing Attack
  4. Brute Force Attack
STAY AWARE, STAY SAFE!!!
